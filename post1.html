Real Estate Price Predictor

This was a project I worked on with my team to create a predictoion algorithm for properties in Pune.

The model was trained using already existing houses that had been sold in different areas of Pune, we included houses of various different sizes and specifications.

The algorithm achieved a total of 88.7% of accuracy in predicting the prices.

We used datasets from various different sites and cleaned them first to train our AI Model and the Model we used was Linear Regression.

This could really prove useful to plan the purchase of a house for investment or even living purporses and that too without having to go to a broker.
