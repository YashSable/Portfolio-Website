Organ Donation Management System

This was a project I worked on with my team to create a system to aid the local hospitals to find and keep track of organs for transplants.

The system stored data on donor, doctor, patients and the hospitals and connected them to each other via a centralised database.

Features like cursor and triggers were added to raise alerts for emergnecies and to indicate the stock of organs.

Doctors could easily assign patients to right donors and check the eligibilty of a donor if the scenario required to do so.
