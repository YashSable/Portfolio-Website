Platform to Help Students in 10th choose their stream

This was a project I worked on with my team to create an android app where students from 10th standard get the best suited stream for them.

This was done by making a basic aptitude test on the app itself and the results will be according answers given by students in the test.

All of this was done on Android Studio and we managed to make the app with all the features and functionality.
